Sentences with contrasting sibilant (S-like) sounds.

Stsêey ts'eyh súu éhtsįį.
My grandfather made a nice canoe.

Súus éł jîiz éł ts'ôgh tah ts'į́ xétsax.
The robin and the jay in the tree are crying.

Êy ts'éhxeh seey tsêdl dat-sęy sínt'eh.
That is that woman's black pocket knife.
