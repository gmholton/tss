Sentences with contrasting palatal (SH-like) sounds.

Nshé'e shétth sheen shíi néh'ęh.
Your (man's) son saw snow in summer.

Shos shłǐig' shax shíi éedah.
The bear is in my dog's house.

Shchâay iin shshax shíi xdéltth'ih.
My grandchildren are staying in my house.
