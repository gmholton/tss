Technical Notes -- ł , l

lateral fricatives

Tanacross <ł> is a voiceless lateral fricative [ɬ], and <l> is a voiced lateral approximant [l]. While <l> is phonetically an approximant, it patterns phonologically as the voiced counterpart to the voiceless lateral fricative, participating in all relevant phonological voicing alternations. Neighboring languages, such as Han, exhibit a voiced lateral fricative corresponding to the Tanacross <l>

Letter <ł> is a semi-voiced fricative. It can generally be described as beginning voiceless and transitioning to a fully voiced fricative.
