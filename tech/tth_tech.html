Technical Notes -- tth , ddh

alveo-dental affricates

Tanacross <tth> and <ddh> are voiceless aspirated and unaspirated alveo-dental affricates [tƟh] and [tƟ], respectively.

At the end of syllables <tth> and <ddh> represent voiceless and voiced affricates [tƟ] and [dð], respectively. In phrase-final position <tth> may be pronounced as a fricative <th>.
